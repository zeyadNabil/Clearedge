Clearedge Safety

Safety and training are given high priority and coordinated from the corporate office.

Regional Operations Training Staff assist with new employee orientation, required technical training, Safety Audits, and mentor new managers.

Clearedge Facility Services, Inc. provides ongoing training with emphasis on safety starting with the highest levels of management all the way down to the janitor who cleans your building after dark. Specific modules of health, safety and environmental are covered every month in training to all Clearedge cleaning technicians.

Our company has over 40 years experience providing a premium level of service to our Fortune 500 and other customers. Now we can assist them in reducing their operating costs by helping with risk management when we are selected as their "Trusted Advisor" in Facility Services.
